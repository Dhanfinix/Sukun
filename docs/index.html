A look inside Sukun

A meticulously crafted interface respecting your serenity.

Granular Automation

Sukun allows you to dictate precisely how long your phone should remain silent for every individual prayer. Need 45 minutes for Friday Jumu'ah but only 15 minutes for Fajr? Adjust the sliders exactly to your local mosque's unique rhythm.

Flexible Manual Mode

Unexpectedly step into a meeting, study circle, or mosque outside of regular prayer times? Trigger a manual timer instantly to silence all media, ringtones, and notifications, letting it automatically restore when you leave.

Absolute Peace

When Sukun takes over, you gain total awareness of the active silence window. A clear red countdown ensures you know exactly when your phone’s volume will return to normal life, with an emergency killswitch at the center.

Beautiful Dark Mode

Respect your eyes in low-light environments. Sukun features a gorgeous, true-deep-black AMOLED theme that seamlessly syncs with your system settings and honors Android's Material You dynamic colors.

How Sukun Works

A seamless experience from setup to daily devotion.

1

Set Location

Provide your location once, and Sukun automatically fetches the most accurate prayer times from the Aladhan API.

2

Precise Scheduling

Using Android's native AlarmManager, Sukun schedules exact alarms for each daily prayer without draining battery.

3

Auto-Silence

When the Adhan rings, your phone instantly switches to silent or vibrate mode according to your preferences.

4

Auto-Restore

After your custom prayer duration ends, Sukun restores your phone's original volume settings automatically.
